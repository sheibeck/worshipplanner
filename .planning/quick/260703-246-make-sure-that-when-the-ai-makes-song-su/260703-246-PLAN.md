<objective>
When the AI suggests songs it must never draw from soft-deleted songs, exactly as it already excludes hidden songs.

**Critical codebase fact (verified during planning):** In this app there is NO separate "deleted" field or collection. `deleteSong()` in `src/stores/songs.ts` performs a soft-delete by setting `hidden: true` (called from `SongSlideOver.vue`). The "Hidden Songs (N)" section in `SongsView.vue` IS the deleted list, and `restoreSong()` un-deletes by setting `hidden: false`. Therefore **soft-deleted === hidden**, and the deleted-song exclusion is achieved through the existing `hidden` filter — do NOT introduce a new field or mechanism.

Both AI entry points in `ServiceEditorView.vue` already filter `songStore.songs.filter((s) => !s.hidden)` inline (comment "D-18: exclude hidden (soft-deleted) songs from AI base"). The remaining risk is that this exclusion is duplicated inline in two places with no shared source of truth and no automated test — so it can silently regress and there is no proof deleted songs stay out of the candidate set.

Purpose: Guarantee (and lock in with a test) that soft-deleted songs can never enter the AI suggestion candidate pool.
Output: A single store getter that owns the non-hidden AI pool, both call sites wired to it, and a unit test proving the exclusion.
</objective>
