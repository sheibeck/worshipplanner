<objective>
Add group-based same-service co-occurrence enforcement and per-(person, role) frequency/tier logic to the deterministic scheduler, TDD-first.

Purpose: `scheduler.ts` today has NO cross-role same-service exclusion and NO access to `Role.group`. Phase 15 makes a person's role holdings on one date subject to group rules: TECH is exclusive; BAND/VOCALS/OTHER combine but capped at 1 BAND and 1 VOCALS. The confirmed landmine (RESEARCH Pitfall 2) is that `propagatePairing` is a second, independent role-selection path â€” the check must be applied in BOTH places via one shared helper or paired partners silently produce illegal combos, breaking D-11's "auto-propose never creates an illegal combo" promise. Additionally, per-role cadence means the deficit formula must score against each role's own N, not a per-person aggregate.

Output: `proposeQuarterSchedule` gains a `roleGroupOf` parameter and a shared exported group-combo helper enforced in both assignment paths; deficit + tier reads become per-role; ProposeResult external shape stays identical. Full TDD coverage in scheduler.test.ts.
</objective>

    - cadence N for scoring = `person.roleFrequencies?.[roleId] ?? person.frequencyTargetN`
