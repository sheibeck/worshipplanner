<objective>
Relocate per-role serve frequency from the standing `Person` tier to the quarter-scoped
`PersonQuarterData.roleFrequency` field (D-04/D-05), seed new quarters from the prior
quarter (D-06), and repoint the CSV volunteer-import pipeline so its Frequency column lands
as per-role `roleFrequency` on the quarter (not a standing `frequencyTargetN` write). This is
the foundation the scheduler fix (R-12), the frequency UI consolidation (R-05), and the
blackout framing (R-06) build on.

Purpose: One frequency, quarter-scoped, seeded from last quarter — eliminates the "frequency
on both roster and schedule" confusion the user flagged, and keeps CSV import writing to that
single source instead of the now-removed standing field.
Output: New `roleFrequency` type field + quarters-store write/seed paths (setPersonAvailability,
createQuarter, applyCsvToQuarter) + CSV parser/modal repoint. Old `Person`/`PersonQuarterData`
frequency fields are left in place as `@deprecated` optional so the build stays green while later
Wave-2 plans repoint their readers; plan 16-11 removes them after all readers migrate.
</objective>
